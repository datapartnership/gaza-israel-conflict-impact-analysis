Observations#

  • The Governorate of Gaza has seen the highest impact for industry and commerce.

  • North Gaza has the second highest amount of roads damaged both in absolute figures and percentage values.

  • While some of the industrial buildings are damaged, a higher percentage of them do not have oberserved nighttime lights. This could slow down economic activity significantly.

  • Critical Infrstructure Points consist of telecommunication towers, drinking water wells and waste treatment plants. The damage to these points could imply a hit on access to drinking water for the people in Gaza.

The chart below shows the percentage of residential buildings retrieved from OpenStreetMap for which the nighttime lights intensity has decreased as of December 2023 by at least 95% compared to September 2023 for each governorate in Gaza.
