Baseline Points of Interest and Conflict in the Gaza Strip#

Assignment#

The purpose of this notebook is to visualize the baseline data that informs damage assessment, and potential impact from the damages to Points of Interest and people.

Methodology#

The data (described in the previous section) was juxtaposed to identify areas in the Gaza Strip where events and fatalities were reported. The Points of Interest available from OpenStreetMaps is also visualized for visual exploration and verification. The Points of Interest visually observed here can be tracked for damages using the subsequent damage assessment.
